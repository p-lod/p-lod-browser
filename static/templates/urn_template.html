Pompeii Artistic Landscape Project (PALP).

P-LOD is jointly directed by Sebastian Heath (NYU) and Eric Poehler (UMass Amherst). It includes data collected for the Getty Foundation funded Pompeii Artistic Landscape Project and the NEH funded Pompeii Bibliography and Mapping Project.
